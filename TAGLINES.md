---

## How to Choose:

Consider these factors:

1. **Target Audience:** Who are your primary clients? (Enterprises, startups, specific industries)
2. **Brand Personality:** Do you want to be seen as innovative, trustworthy, technical, or partnership-focused?
3. **Differentiation:** What sets you apart from competitors?
4. **Memorability:** Which tagline will stick in people's minds?
5. **Flexibility:** Does it work across all your service offerings?

## Implementation:

To change the tagline in your website:
1. Open `index.html`
2. Find the hero subtitle section (around line 56)
3. Update the `<p class="hero-subtitle">` content
4. Update the footer tagline in `<p class="footer-tagline">` (around line 422)
5. Save and refresh your browser
